About InsectiScan

InsectiScan is an advanced AI-powered application designed to accurately identify and diagnose insect bites, providing comprehensive risk analysis and treatment recommendations. Our sophisticated image recognition technology helps users understand potential health implications and appropriate care steps.

Beyond insect bites, our platform extends its capabilities to identifying various plants and animals, making it a versatile tool for outdoor enthusiasts and anyone concerned about potential environmental hazards.
