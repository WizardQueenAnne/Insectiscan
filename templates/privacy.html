PRIVACY POLICY:

At InsectiScan, we respect your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data, images, or usage information. All scans are processed locally on your device, and no data is transmitted to our servers or any third parties. We do not track your activity, request personal information, or use analytics tools. Your use of the app is completely private. By using InsectiScan, you can rest assured that your data stays on your device and is never accessed by us. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at INSECTISCAN@GMAIL.COM.
